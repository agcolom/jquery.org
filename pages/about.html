The jQuery Foundation is a member supported non-profit trade association for web developers. We:

  • Participate in the web standards process as an advocate for the needs of web developers, through groups such as the W3C and ECMA TC39 (JavaScript).
  • Provide a safe, neutral home for our many open source projects and want to hear from projects looking for a home.
  • Host world-wide conferences and developer events where developers can learn and share their experiences in creating applications.

For more information, see our mission & vision statement.
